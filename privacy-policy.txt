This Policy applies to all visitors and users of our Services. By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge and consent to the practices described in this Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to operate and improve the Services, including:

* Basic identifiers (such as email address if provided)

## 3. Automated and AI-Assisted Processing

No Limit Flix® uses AI-assisted and rule-based systems to interpret user inputs and generate content discovery recommendations. These processes are session-based and do not rely on long-term behavioral profiling.

Automated systems are used solely to improve relevance, usability, and platform performance and are designed with transparency and human oversight in mind.
